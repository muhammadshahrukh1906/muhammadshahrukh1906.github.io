3. Strategic SWOT Analysis Matrix Framework

OVERALL UNDERSTANDING: Millat tractor is a leading distributor agricultural equipment in Pakistan. It is also a distributor of AGCO (major global manufacturer and distributor of agricultural equipment and technology). The company's financial ratios indicate good profits due to high operational efficiency but also high financial debt.
STRENGTHS Internal Asset
STRONG PROFITABILITY MARGINS "Increasing gross, operating and net profitability margins indicate strong cost control and effective pricing because of which it can earn higher profits which can be used to increase EPS As well as increased profits when sales volume increase"
SHAREHOLDER'S RETURNS "Increasing ROE AND ROA reflect increasing shareholder returns from equity and assets which means that the company is using shareholder's money efficiently to generate money"
MARKET VALUE "High market book ratio indicates high market confidence in the company and strong expectations about its future growth possibly due to high ROE and ROA"
WEAKNESSES Internal Constraint
WEAK LIQUIDITY "Current and quick ratio is below the industry's reflecting solvency risk. Low quick ratio reflects heavy reliance upon inventory in assets and stress upon the company if a cash flow disruption occurs"
INEFFICIENT INVENTORY MANAGEMENT "Low inventory turnover reflects suggests inconsistent inefficient inventory management caused by inefficient purchasing, weak sales and high inventory costs"
HIGH DEBT "Company is using more debt that the industry to carry out its operations creating high financial risk and pressure in times where income is variable but it has high inventory so it is possible it's inventory is growing"
OPPORTUNITIES External Vector
INDUSTRY OPPORTUNITY "Increasing farmer's incomes, government subsidies along with some favorable economic conditions could increase tractors sales and promote growth along with better profit margins"
MARKET EXPAND "Millat has high profitability ratios than its competitors meaning it could capture more market share than its competitors especially if the competition weakens because of increase in input costs"
LEVERAGE OPERATIONAL EFFICIENCY "Since Millat has high efficiency in its operations it could leverage this advantage because if sales increase then profits could increase exponentially due to cost saving operations"
THREATS External Risk
COMPETITOR PRICING POWER "If competitors decrease their power then Millat too will be forced to decrease its prices which it may be able to do because of its efficient cost saving operations but its profits will be lowered"
CHANGES IN GOVERNMENT POLICY "A change in government subsidies and taxation programs could lower tractor sales volume which will negatively affect profitability, activity ratios and will be now in high debt with lower sales"
ECONOMIC CRISIS "A decrease in farmers' income will decrease total sales volume that would worsen revenue, and inventory turnover leading to excess inventory while also decreasing profit margins"

Big companies have built brand recognition and customer loyalty among farmers which is a sort of barrier to entry along with an oligopoly.
